How to Play

  1. Gather your friends or play solo.

  3. Answer the trivia questions to the best of your knowledge.
  4. Keep track of your score.
  5. Play fair - no peeking at the answers!
  6. Take breaks and stay hydrated.
  7. Have fun and good luck!
